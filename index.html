Welcome to Financial Calculations

This website helps you calculate key financial metrics related to the time value of money. Below are the main calculations you can perform:

Available Calculations

  • FVIF (Future Value Interest Factor): Calculate the future value of a single sum.
  • FVIFA (Future Value Interest Factor for an Annuity): Calculate the future value of a series of equal payments (annuity).
  • PVIF (Present Value Interest Factor): Calculate the present value of a single sum to be received in the future.
  • PVIFA (Present Value Interest Factor for an Annuity): Calculate the present value of a series of equal payments (annuity).

Example Usage

Suppose you want to calculate the future value of $1,000 invested at an annual interest rate of 5% for 10 years. You can use the FVIF Calculator by entering an interest rate of 5% and a period of 10 years. The calculator will show you how much your $1,000 will grow to in 10 years.
